About Lisa

Lisa Cohen, MS, PT, is a licensed physical therapist with expertise in orthopedic conditions, sports injuries, and women’s health. She works with the Greater Jerusalem Swim team and Olympic athletes, using manual and holistic techniques to ease pain. Lisa also specializes in orthotics evaluations with advanced 3D scanning and applies methods like fascial manipulation, craniosacral therapy, muscle energy, trigger point release, and kinesiotaping.
