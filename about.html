About Lisa

Background, expertise, and approach

Lisa Cohen MS, PT

Professional Background

Lisa Cohen is a licensed Physical Therapist, holding her certification since 1983. She earned her master’s degree in Exercise Physiology in 1991, both degrees conferred by the City University of New York—her PT degree from Hunter College and her master’s from Queens College.

Areas of Expertise

Lisa specializes in treating a variety of conditions, including orthopedic concerns such as back pain, headaches, and sports injuries, as well as pre- and post-partum issues. She serves as the physical therapist for swimmers and triathletes on the Greater Jerusalem Swim team and has extensive experience working with Olympic swimmers.

Treatment Approach

She employs a unique blend of manual techniques aimed at relieving her patients’ pain efficiently. Each individual is assessed holistically, with careful consideration for both physical and emotional well-being.

Orthotics Evaluation

Lisa conducts comprehensive assessments for prescription orthotics and has partnered for many years with Adam 3D Orthotics. She utilizes state-of-the-art technology for foot evaluation, including a 3D scanner from Aetrex, a leading American company, ensuring each orthotic is crafted with the highest precision.

Specialized Treatment Techniques

  • Fascial Manipulation – Levels 1, 2, and 3
  • Craniosacral Therapy
  • Muscle Energy Techniques
  • Trigger Point Release
  • Kinesiotaping
